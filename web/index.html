PILOT

A Claude Code plugin that auto-routes your intent to the right underlying skill, enforces CLAUDE.md quality gates, and stays extensible with a one-line registry edit.

what
Six shell hooks + a routing skill + six slash commands + a bundled context7 MCP, wired automatically by the plugin manifest.
how
SessionStart prints a banner. PreToolUse gates large edits and bad commits. Stop / SubagentStop verify "done" claims. PreCompact re-anchors routing rules.
why
So your AI sessions look the same across framing, planning, TDD, debugging, and shipping — without remembering which skill to invoke.
